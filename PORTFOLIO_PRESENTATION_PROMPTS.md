## Prompt 1 - General Portfolio Deck

You are a career positioning consultant and product storyteller.

I am uploading materials from my CV and portfolio. Help me create a concise presentation titled:

Daniil Velikanov - AI Operations & HR Automation Portfolio

My goal is to show myself not as a regular HR specialist and not as a junior developer, but as a practical specialist at the intersection of HR, operations, AI automation, browser agents, documents, spreadsheets, and business workflows.

Create an 8-10 slide structure:

1. Who I am
2. My positioning
3. Business problems I solve
4. Proof from experience and numbers
5. Case 1: AI Lead Triage Dashboard
6. Case 2: HR Automation Scenarios
7. Case 3: SEO Product Description Generator
8. Side project: Digital Detox
9. Tools and workflow stack
10. Contacts and what I am looking for

Style: minimalist, clean, dark or neutral visual direction, no filler, short phrases, business-readable. Write in simple professional English.

Important positioning:

- Do not present me as a senior developer.
- Present me as an AI Operations / HR Automation specialist.
- Emphasize practical workflows, business value, human-in-the-loop automation, and fast prototypes.
